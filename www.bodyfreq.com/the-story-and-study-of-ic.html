The story of the discovery and study of the phenomenon of IC - Information copies of substances


What is an information copy (IC) of the substance?

The spectral characteristics of the substances can be "saved" by various technical means transferred to the medium and transferred to the body. As a result, the response of the body against the input IC copy of the substance is similar to his reaction to the substance itself.

French scientist Jacques Benveniste. In 1988 he proved the existence of "water memory". In 1991. first the transfer of IC copies of substances off.

Nobel Laureate Luc Montagnier in 2009 confirmed the possibility of transferring the spectral characteristics of the DNA into the water.

In 2004, radio physicist Mark Greenstein and Dr. Michael Shraybman developed techniques for preparing and transmitting IC copies. Since 2009, the technology developed by the fund DST, which brings together 28 doctors and 12 researchers. It was demonstrated that the IC copies no adverse health effects.

The technology is patented IC Medicals: PCT / RU 2012/000033, WO 2013/112066 A2

Technology IC Medicals today

The Technical center of IC Medicals
The technical information center now contains 60 copies of a variety of substances linked to human health.

All computers, laptops, tablets, phones - themselves are emitters signal. By transmitting information copies off through them people can now seamlessly use technology IC Medicals, without buying anything extra. For higher performance and reliability, can be used IC transmitter connected via Bluetooth or an audio cable.

IC information copies can be stored for different times of data media, water, etc.
